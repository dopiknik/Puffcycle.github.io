FAQ

How does PuffCycle work? +

Once you request a bin, our team will deliver it directly to your location. When the bin is full, we’ll return to collect it and ensure all items are responsibly recycled. No shipping, no hassle—just simple, convenient recycling.

How quickly can I get a bin delivered? +

Standard delivery is within 24-48 hours. Same-day delivery is available for urgent requests with an additional fee.

Do I have to pay for the bin or pickup? +

Nope. The bin, delivery, pickup, and recycling are all free. There’s no cost to you.

How can I contact support? +

Call us at (555) 123-4567, email [email protected], or use the contact form. Our team is available 7 days a week.
