Vape Waste: Hidden Environmental Threat

Millions of disposable vapes end up in landfills every day, poisoning soil with lithium-ion batteries and toxic chemicals. A single discarded vape can pollute groundwater over an area the size of a football field for up to 50 years.

Your Puffcycle Donation Creates Real Impact

Every $10 allows us to safely process 15 vape devices, recover valuable metals for reuse, and keep toxins out of nature. Your support directly protects the ecosystem for future generations.

Technology of the future today

We use advanced recycling methods: specialized robots disassemble devices and recover 98% of materials for reuse. Old vapes become new batteries for EVs and solar systems.

Join the movement

You become part of a global eco-activist community. Your name is added to an honor roll of planet defenders, and every month you receive a report detailing the impact of your contribution with photos and stories of protected areas.

FAQ

How does PuffCycle work? +

Once you request a bin, our team will drop it off at your location. When it’s full, we’ll swing by to pick it up and handle all the recycling — no shipping, no hassle.

How quickly can I get a bin delivered? +

Standard delivery is within 24-48 hours. Same-day delivery is available for urgent requests with an additional fee.

Do I have to pay for the bin or pickup? +

Nope. The bin, delivery, pickup, and recycling are all free. There’s no cost to you.

What can be put in the bin? +

You can toss in any disposable vape devices, cartridges, and pods — even if they still contain e-liquid or batteries. We handle the safe, responsible recycling of all components.
